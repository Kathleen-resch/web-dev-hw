The Colors of the 2000s

The 2000s were an iconic time in the history of pop culture and fashion. The pallette of the 2000s can be charachterized by bright colors, especially blue and red tones. Pink and the blue of blue jeans were the touchstones of fashion. These iconic fashion trends can be seen in the fashion of three female icons: Paris Hilton, Britney Spears, and Elle Woods from the movie Legally Blonde .
