Why I chose the topic I chose

I chose to make my instructable on tuning a ukelele because I have recently started learning to play the ukelele. Learning to tune it was one of the first things I had to learn how to do. You need a tuned ukelele in order properly play it. I have been learning to ply the ukelele because I have always loved music and playing the ukelele has become a new hobby of mine. It's a big stress reliever for me.

If you want to learn more I was inspired by the wiki-how article about this subject as well as a variety of videos on Youtube.
