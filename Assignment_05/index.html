Dill Dip


Here is Another recipie for Dill Dip this one is based off of

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Mayonnaise
  • 1 cup of Sour Cream
  • 1 teaspoon of Dry Dill
  • 1 teaspoon of Parsley
  • 1 teaspoon of Beau Monde
  • A splash of Pickle Juice

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. In a mixing bowl add an equal amount of Mayonaise and Sour Cream
  3. Add a teaspoon of Dry dill, Parsley, and Beau Mond
  4. Stir ingredients until well mixed
  5. Add a splash of pickle juice. Dill Pickle juice works best for this recipie
  6. Taste and add more ingredients if necessary
  7. For best results chill for 2 hours
  8. Serve with Pretzels, Potato chips, or Vegetables
