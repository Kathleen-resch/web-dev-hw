William Shakepeare

William Shakespeare is one of the most famous British playwrights. He was born in 1564 and raised in Stratford-upon-Avon. His sonnets were written throughout his carrer and were published in 1609. Shakespeare wrote 154 sonnets for a variety of lovers. Historians have speculated his sonnets are about two people, a dark married woman and a young man. Shakespeare also wrote 39 plays in a variety of genres. His works have been published in almost every modern language and are still performed today. William Shakespeare died in 1616.
